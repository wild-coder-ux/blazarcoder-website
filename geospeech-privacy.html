Information We Collect

✅

GeoSpeech does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app does not require you to create an account, provide your name, email address, phone number, or any other identifying information. No analytics, crash reporting, or usage tracking is implemented.

2

Permissions Used

GeoSpeech requests the following Android permissions. Each one is used solely to power a core feature of the app:


Permission Name Why it's needed
INTERNET Internet Access To load map tiles (CARTO), look up place names (Nominatim/OSM), and fetch geography facts (Wikipedia). All connections use HTTPS.
RECORD_AUDIO Microphone To allow you to speak place names using the built-in speech recognition. Audio is processed entirely on-device by the Android Web Speech API — it is never sent to our servers.
MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS Audio Settings Required alongside microphone access to enable speech recognition and text-to-speech features on Android.
ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE Network State To check whether the device is online before attempting to load maps or fetch data, so the app can gracefully handle offline situations.
3

Third-Party Services

GeoSpeech uses the following free, open-source third-party services to deliver its features. These services may receive the place names you search for as part of normal network requests. We do not share any personal data with them because we do not collect any.


🗺
CARTO Basemaps
Provides map tiles. Requests include your approximate tile coordinates (map area you are viewing). No personal data is sent. Privacy policy: carto.com/privacy
FREE / OSS
📍
Nominatim (OpenStreetMap)
Converts place names you type or speak into map coordinates. Requests include the place name text only. No personal data is sent. Privacy policy: osmfoundation.org/policies/privacy
FREE / OSS
📖
Wikipedia API
Fetches short geography facts for places you look up. Requests include only the place name. No personal data is sent. Privacy policy: foundation.wikimedia.org/privacy-policy
FREE / OSS
🎙
Android Web Speech API
Processes your voice input for speech recognition and text-to-speech. This is a built-in Android system feature. Audio processing behaviour is governed by your device manufacturer and Google's privacy policy.
BUILT-IN
4

Data Storage

GeoSpeech stores your session data (the places you have added during a session) only in your device's local browser/WebView storage. This data never leaves your device and is cleared when you reset the app or clear app data.

We do not operate any servers or databases. There is no backend infrastructure associated with GeoSpeech.

5

Children's Privacy

GeoSpeech is an educational geography app suitable for all ages, including children. Because we collect no personal data whatsoever, the app is safe for use by children under 13 (COPPA) and children under 16 (GDPR). No parental consent is required to use this app.

6

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Since GeoSpeech collects no personal data, changes are unlikely to affect your privacy in any meaningful way.
